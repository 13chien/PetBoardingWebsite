Most affordable Pet Boarding!


The Pet Boarder first started out in 2012 by boarding Hamsters at home. By the end of 2013, we started boarding Rabbits, Guinea Pigs, Terrapins and other small pets!

We are very experienced in pet boarding and are willing to meet your every special needs and request! Recently in 2019, we have also open up to the option of dog sitting services at the doggie's own home.
Pet boarding is done in a homely environment which means you can rest assure your treasured pets are in a quiet and safe space. Boarding pets at home also reduces the cost for a space rental and hence thats why we are able to provide pet boarding services at such affordable prices.
We also provide pick up and drop off services for your pets amongst our other services. You can find out more about the services we provide by clicking our respective Services. Alternatively if you have any inquiry, feel free to contact us by dropping us a text, whatsapp or email!
We hope to hear from you soon!
